Figure 4.4 — A three-story building of aggregation

Each floor compresses the floor below it. Cohen's d is the unit that travels — and so is whatever bias it carried at floor 1.

Roughly 130,000 individual studies → ~2,100 meta-analyses → 1 meta-meta-analysis. Approximately 300 million students sit at the base of the stack (MetaX 1.3, late 2024).
