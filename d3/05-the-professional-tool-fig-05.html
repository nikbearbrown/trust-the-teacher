Figure 5.5 — Three objects, not two

Policy keeps asking "with-AI vs. without-AI" — and averaging two distinct operator conditions inside the "with" column.

Three operator conditions live behind the binary framing: the trained teacher with AI, the untrained teacher with AI, and the trained teacher without AI. Studies that pool the first two report a small effect — not because the tool is weak, but because the average is hiding the trained condition inside the untrained one.
