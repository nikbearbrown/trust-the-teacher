Figure 10.3 — The Carrell-West reversal

Air Force Academy random assignment, common downstream finals: evaluations tracked the first-course grade and inverted the downstream signal.

Less-experienced instructors earned higher Calc I grades and higher student evaluations; experienced PhD faculty earned lower Calc I grades and lower evaluations, but their students did better in Calc II and Calc III. The instrument rewarded the instructors whose students were comfortable, not the ones whose students were learning.
