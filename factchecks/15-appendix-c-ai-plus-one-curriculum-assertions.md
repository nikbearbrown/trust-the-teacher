## Single Sharpest Factual Problem

**The Finland tutoropettaja "23 million euros across the government period" figure on lines 13 and 119.** The chapter cites €7.5M as the initial 2016 allocation (CONFIRMED) and €23M as the total across the government period. However, available sources indicate the cumulative funding across 2016–2019 was approximately **€34 million** (distributed through OPH state grants to ~350 of Finland's ~400 education providers). The €23M figure may reflect a shorter time window or exclude certain categories of funding, but as currently stated it appears to understate the actual cumulative Finnish investment by roughly a third. Since the chapter's full-version cost argument (the "$100M/yr for CA/TX" claim on line 119) is partially anchored to this Finnish figure as a per-capita benchmark, an undercount here propagates downstream into the U.S. cost estimate. **Recommend** the author pull the OPH Faktaa Express 4A/2020 (or 3C/2018 English brief) and pin the cumulative figure precisely, then re-derive the U.S. scaling estimate from the corrected base.
