## Top flags requiring author attention before drafting

1. **Ch 1 — the headline opening number.** TIKTOC says "30% suspension increase for Black boys, near zero for white/Hispanic." NBER digest reports 16% for Black students *overall*. The race × sex subgroup table from Figlio & Özek WP 34388 needs to be verified directly before that opening sentence is written.
2. **Ch 1 — state count is moving.** "31 states" as of TIKTOC drafting; Kansas became #33 in March 2026. Pin definition (bell-to-bell statutory? guidance? K-8 only?) and date.
3. **Ch 2 — the "+0.28 vs −0.57 SD" pairing has no single peer-reviewed source.** Recommend rewriting as narrative comparison anchored to Ma et al. (+0.41) and Bastani (−17%, with SD calculation shown).
4. **Ch 4 — Hattie effect sizes have shifted on MetaX.** CTE d=1.57 is contested and currently in flux; feedback has been revised from d=0.73 to d=0.50. The chapter should use current values and acknowledge instability.
5. **Ch 5 — "Kansas Farm Efficiency Study" is a label, not a citation.** Two possible underlying studies (Purdue-KFMA Fiechter et al. vs. Griffin/Shockley/Mark) — pick one by author name.
6. **Ch 6 — 5.9 hr/wk is self-report, not observed time.** Methodologically defensible (trimmed, weighted) but the EEF RCT provides the only causal cover. Position the dividend as creating slack, not as a productivity guarantee.
7. **Ch 7 — Bastani heterogeneity-by-self-regulation is an inference, not a paper finding.** The pre-registered moderator analysis showed limited statistical heterogeneity. The equity chain (Bastani + Evans-Schamberg + Blair-Raver) must be labeled as an inference, not as Bastani's direct claim.
8. **Ch 8 — WestEd 30/70 rule has no primary WestEd citation.** Reframe or drop the attribution.
9. **Ch 8 — Finnish DigiErko's last cohort ended December 2024.** Do not write in active present tense.
10. **Ch 9 — no district is running a branded "Phone Fridays" program.** This is Brown's design pattern, not a citation. The chapter has to own the prescriptive framing rather than claim documented success.
11. **Ch 9 + Ch 12 — Irreducibly Human Tier 1/Tier 4 + seven-tier taxonomy attribution.** Appendix E references a seven-tier taxonomy at irreduciblyhuman.xyz that the public site (irreducibly.xyz) doesn't currently list — five courses listed instead. Reconcile before Appendix E publishes a URL.
12. **Ch 10 — "0.27 GPA-point inflation" sourced only to a 2025 non-peer-reviewed preprint.** Find peer-reviewed sourcing or replace with the Stroebe 2020 *Educational Psychologist* analysis.
13. **Ch 12 — the vignette teacher is not a real named person.** Recommendation: pull a named Edutopia/NEA/EdSurge profile or explicitly frame as composite.
