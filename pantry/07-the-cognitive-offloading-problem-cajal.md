## Zone Map

- **MC:** The three-event biological chain: prediction error → effortful retrieval → schema construction → durable memory. Unguided AI bypasses all three.
- **VG:** The fluency trap — what learning *feels* like is not what learning *is*. The inverse coupling needs to be visible.
- **PQ:** Bastani 2025 results — +48% practice gain (GPT Base), −17% exam decrement (GPT Base), ~0% exam decrement (GPT Tutor). Same model, different scaffolding, opposite-sign outcomes.
